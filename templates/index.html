About RentFair Homes

We're building Nigeria's most transparent, trustworthy, and accessible property marketplace — starting from Ekiti State.

Our mission

RentFair Homes was founded to solve a real problem in the Nigerian property market: lack of price transparency, fake listings, and the frustrating disconnect between renters/buyers and genuine property owners.

We believe every Nigerian deserves access to verified, fairly priced homes — without the stress, deception, or hidden costs that plague the current market.

Our values

  • Transparency — No hidden fees, no vague listings, no misleading photos.
  • Fairness — Price insights that empower renters and buyers.
  • Trust — Every listing verified before it goes live.
  • Access — Connecting Nigerians with the right homes at fair prices.
